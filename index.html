Good to Know

What counts as a "revision round"?

One revision round = one batch of change requests submitted together after reviewing a completed design or build stage. Individual edits within that batch don't count separately. Revisions submitted piecemeal over multiple days count as separate rounds.

When do I pay?

We’ll confirm payment timing in writing before we start.

What if I need to cancel mid-project?

Deposits are non-refundable once design work has started. If you cancel after the build phase begins, you're responsible for work completed to date.

Who owns the final site?

You do. All custom code, designs, and content you provided are yours at launch. Licensed fonts, images, or plugins transfer under their original licenses.

Do you offer rush delivery?

Yes. Rush fee is 25% of the package price for timelines under the standard window.

What does "SEO" mean in these packages?

Technical on-page SEO only: meta tags, schema, site structure, speed, and mobile optimization. Ongoing SEO services, link building, and content marketing are separate engagements.

What browsers do you support?

The latest two versions of Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge. Legacy browser support (IE11, old Android) is available as a paid add-on.
